# CVIA CRM Architecture Case Study

## Situation

CVIA was building a regional innovation ecosystem spanning entrepreneurs, investors, advisors, educators, and public-sector leaders.

The organization required a scalable operational platform capable of supporting rapid growth while maintaining visibility into relationships, projects, and ecosystem activity.

## Task

Design and implement a centralized system capable of:

- Managing stakeholders
- Tracking ecosystem engagement
- Supporting events
- Coordinating projects
- Providing leadership reporting

## Action

Conducted stakeholder analysis and requirements gathering.

Designed a relational CRM architecture in Airtable.

Created:

- Contact management systems
- Organization databases
- Project tracking workflows
- Event management processes
- Reporting dashboards

Implemented automation workflows to reduce manual effort and improve consistency.

## Result

The system supported:

- 200+ stakeholders
- 1,000+ changemakers
- 9 participating cities
- 27 startup companies
- 54 advisors and mentors

The CRM became the operational backbone supporting CVIA's Pilot initiative and future ecosystem growth.
