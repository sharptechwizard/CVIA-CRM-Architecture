# CVIA CRM Architecture

A systems architecture and CRM implementation project developed for the Coachella Valley Innovation Alliance (CVIA).

## Overview

The Coachella Valley Innovation Alliance is a regional initiative focused on connecting entrepreneurs, investors, mentors, educational institutions, civic leaders, and community organizations across the Coachella Valley.

To support this mission, I designed and implemented a centralized CRM and operational management platform using Airtable.

The platform provides:

- Stakeholder relationship management
- Event management
- Project tracking
- Mentor and advisor coordination
- Investor engagement
- Community reporting
- Workflow automation
- Operational documentation

## Business Challenge

Prior to implementation, stakeholder information, event planning, project tracking, and ecosystem relationships were distributed across multiple systems and manual processes.

CVIA required a scalable solution capable of:

- Managing a rapidly growing innovation ecosystem
- Tracking relationships between organizations and individuals
- Supporting multi-city collaboration
- Streamlining communications
- Providing executive-level visibility into ecosystem activity

## Solution

Designed and implemented a relational Airtable CRM platform consisting of interconnected databases supporting:

- 200+ stakeholders
- 1,000+ changemakers
- 9 cities
- 27 startup companies
- 54 advisors and mentors
- Multiple public and private sector partners

## Key Contributions

### Systems Architecture

Designed the overall CRM structure, governance model, data relationships, and operational workflows.

### Database Design

Created relational database architecture supporting organizations, contacts, projects, events, mentors, investors, and ecosystem engagement.

### Workflow Automation

Implemented automated processes for:

- Stakeholder onboarding
- Event registration
- Follow-up activities
- Reporting workflows
- Data quality management

### Reporting & Visibility

Developed dashboards and reporting structures providing leadership with actionable insights into ecosystem growth and engagement.
